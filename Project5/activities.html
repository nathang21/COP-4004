Atlantic Trails Resprt has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Atlantic Trails, we offer guided birdwatching tips at sunrise several times a week.
